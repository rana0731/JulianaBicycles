Margaux Elliott rode her Maverick 6,292 miles over the course of about 1,000 hours and 253 days to accomplish her goal of climbing ONE MILLION feet in 2020. “Even though there was as much descending as climbing, I always referred to it as a challenge to climb one million feet because climbing was the thing I was always bad at. Focusing on something that had always been a weakness of mine and overcoming the challenge has been empowering and gave me confidence both on and off the bike.” You can read Margaux’s whole interview on Pinkbike with the link in our bio.
